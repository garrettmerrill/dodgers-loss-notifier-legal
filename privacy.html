Privacy Policy

Dodgers Loss Notifier

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide SMS game notifications:

  • Mobile phone number — provided directly by recipients who have explicitly opted in to receive notifications.
  • First name — used to personalize messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your mobile phone number and name are used solely to send you SMS notifications about Los Angeles Dodgers game results. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any other purpose.

Consent and Opt-In

You must provide explicit consent before receiving any SMS messages from Dodgers Loss Notifier. Consent is obtained when you voluntarily provide your mobile phone number to the service operator and confirm your desire to receive game result notifications.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose your mobile phone number or personal information to any third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Your phone number is transmitted only to Twilio, our SMS delivery provider, solely for the purpose of delivering messages to you.

Data Storage and Security

Recipient information is stored securely using encrypted environment variables on Amazon Web Services (AWS). We do not store message history or any data beyond what is necessary to deliver notifications. Access to your data is restricted to the service operator only.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your information is retained only while you are an active subscriber. Upon opting out or requesting deletion, your phone number and name will be removed within 24 hours. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at earmarktwain@gmail.com.

Opting Out

You may opt out of notifications at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message (you may also reply STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT)
  • Replying HELP for assistance
  • Emailing us at earmarktwain@gmail.com

Upon opting out, you will receive a confirmation message and no further messages will be sent.

Message Frequency and Rates

Message frequency varies based on the Dodgers' game schedule (typically up to 1 message per game day during the MLB season, April through October). Message and data rates may apply.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, please contact us at earmarktwain@gmail.com.
