Privacy Policy

Dodgers Loss Notifier

Last updated: March 25, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide SMS game notifications:

  • Phone numbers of recipients who have opted in to receive notifications.
  • First names of recipients, used to personalize messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number and name are used solely to send you SMS notifications about MLB game results. We do not use your information for any other purpose.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your phone number is transmitted to Twilio, our SMS delivery provider, solely for the purpose of delivering messages to you.

Data Storage

Recipient information is stored securely in environment variables on AWS. We do not store message history or any data beyond what is needed to deliver notifications.

Opting Out

You may opt out of notifications at any time by replying STOP to any message or by contacting us directly. Your information will be removed promptly upon request.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact the operator of this service directly.
