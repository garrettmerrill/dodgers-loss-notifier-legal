Service Description

Dodgers Loss Notifier is a personal SMS notification service that sends text messages to opted-in recipients when the Los Angeles Dodgers lose a baseball game.

Message Types

You will receive SMS notifications containing MLB game results, specifically Los Angeles Dodgers final scores when the team loses.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on the Dodgers' game schedule. During the MLB season (April through October), you may receive up to 1 message per game day. No messages are sent during the off-season. Message and data rates may apply.

By providing your mobile phone number to Dodgers Loss Notifier, you expressly consent to receiving recurring SMS messages about MLB game results. Consent is not a condition of any purchase or service.

Opting Out

You may stop receiving messages at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any message (you may also reply STOPALL, UNSUBSCRIBE, CANCEL, END, or QUIT)
  • Replying HELP for assistance
  • Emailing us at earmarktwain@gmail.com

After opting out, you will receive a single confirmation message. No further messages will be sent unless you re-subscribe.
